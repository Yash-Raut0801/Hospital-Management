About Ruby Hall Clinic

Pune's Ruby Hall Clinic group of hospitals.

For about 50 years, it has been at the forefront of the healthcare industry, setting unprecedented benchmarks.

Renowned in Pune for exceptional treatments and surgeries in heart, cancer, neurotrauma and transplant.

With a commitment to excellence, advanced facilities and a skilled team, Ruby Hall continues to redefine healthcare standards.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To continue to grow and challenge conventions with our pioneering spirit and forward-thinking leadership while providing exceptional patient care.

  • To grow with innovation while providing exceptional patient care.
  • To be a healthcare destination where technology meets compassion.
